Recipe: Krabby Patty

Description:

The Krabby Patty is the iconic burger from the popular animated series SpongeBob SquarePants.
Learn how to make this underwater delicacy and satisfy your inner Bikini Bottom cravings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Krabby Patty bun
  • 1 Krabby Patty patty (secret formula)
  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • 1 slice of tomato
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 slice of pickles
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon chopped onions
  • Sea salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Start by grilling the Krabby Patty patty on medium heat until it's perfectly browned on both sides.
  2. While the patty is grilling, toast the Krabby Patty bun lightly on the grill.
  3. Assemble your Krabby Patty in this order: bottom bun, lettuce, tomato, cheese, patty, pickles, ketchup, mustard, chopped onions, and top bun.
  4. Season with a pinch of sea salt, and your Krabby Patty is ready to serve!
